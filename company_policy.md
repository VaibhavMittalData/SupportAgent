# ShopWave Support Knowledge Base

## 1. Return Policy
**Standard Returns**
* **Window:** 30 days from the delivery date.
* **Condition:** Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in original condition.
* **Requirement:** Proof of purchase (Order ID) is mandatory.

**Category-Specific Windows**
* **Electronics Accessories** (cables, mounts, stands): 60 days.
* **High-Value Electronics** (laptops, tablets, smartwatches): 15 days.
* **Footwear:** 30 days (must be unworn with absolutely no outdoor use).
* **Sports & Fitness:** 30 days (non-returnable if used due to hygiene policies).

**Non-Returnable Items**
* Items registered online post-purchase (e.g., activated smart devices).
* Perishable goods.
* Digital content or downloadable software.
* Items marked "Final Sale."

**Exceptions (Faults & Errors)**
* **Damaged/Defective on Arrival:** Eligible for a full refund or replacement regardless of the return window. Requires photo evidence. Physical return of the item is usually not required (subject to agent discretion).
* **Wrong Item Delivered:** ShopWave covers return pickup and ships the correct item for free. If out of stock, a full refund is issued. This process does not count against the standard return window.

## 2. Refund Policy
**General Rules**
* All refunds must be verified via the `check_refund_eligibility` tool.
* Issued strictly to the original payment method (irreversible once processed).
* Processing Time: 5–7 business days post-approval.

**Partial Refunds & Fees**
* Partial refunds apply to items returned in non-original condition.
* A 10% restocking fee applies to High-Value Electronics returned after 7 days.

**Customer Tier Leniency**
* **Standard:** Strict adherence to policy. No exceptions.
* **Premium:** Agents may use judgment for borderline cases (e.g., 1–3 days past the window). Requires a supervisor note.
* **VIP:** Maximum leniency. Agents must check customer notes for pre-approvals before ever declining a request.

## 3. Warranty Policy
**Coverage Summary**
* Covers manufacturing defects only.
* Excludes: physical/user damage, water damage, and unauthorized modifications.

**Warranty Periods**
* **Home Appliances:** 24 months from delivery.
* **Standard Electronics:** 12 months from delivery.
* **Electronics Accessories:** 6 months from delivery.
* **Footwear & Sports:** No warranty (governed solely by the Return Policy).

**Claim Process**
* Requires Order ID and a description of the defect.
* Agents must verify eligibility using the delivery date and product category.
* **Action:** Agents do NOT resolve warranty claims directly. Escalate all valid claims to the Warranty Team (Options: repair, replacement, or refund).

## 4. Order Cancellations
* **Processing Status:** Free to cancel at any time. Verified via email within 1 hour.
* **Shipped/Delivered Status:** Cannot be canceled. The customer must wait for delivery and initiate a standard return.

## 5. Exchange Policy
* Applies only to wrong size, wrong color, or wrong item delivered.
* Strictly subject to stock availability (if out of stock, default to a full refund).
* Processing an exchange does not extend the original return window.

## 6. Customer Tiers & Verification
* **CRITICAL:** Customer tiers must be verified exclusively via the `get_customer` tool. Self-declared statuses are invalid and should be flagged.
* **Standard:** Standard policies apply.
* **Premium:** Priority queue; agent discretion permitted for borderline cases.
* **VIP:** Dedicated support; highest leniency; check notes for management pre-approvals.

## 7. Escalation Protocol
**When to Escalate to a Human Agent:**
1. All Warranty claims.
2. Requests involving replacing (not refunding) a damaged item.
3. Conflicting data between customer claims and system records.
4. Refunds exceeding $200.
5. Suspected fraud, manipulation, or social engineering.
6. Resolutions requiring supervisor approval (e.g., Premium tier borderline exceptions).
7. Agent confidence score drops below 0.6.

**Required Escalation Details:**
When escalating, the ticket must include:
* A concise issue summary.
* Actions already attempted or verified by the system/agent.
* The recommended path to resolution.
* Priority Level (Low / Medium / High / Urgent).

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
* **How long does a refund take?** 5–7 business days after approval (bank processing times vary).
* **Can I return a product I've used?** Generally no. Goods must be unused. (Exceptions exist for defects/damage).
* **Can I get a refund without returning the item?** Only permitted for cases of damage on arrival or manufacturing defect confirmed by the support team.
* **What if I don't have my order number?** Agents can look up the order using the customer's registered email address.
* **Do you offer free returns?** Yes, for wrong items delivered and damaged/defective items. Standard returns may incur a return shipping fee depending on the reason.
* **Can I exchange instead of getting a refund?** Yes, subject to stock availability. See the Exchange Policy section above.

## 9. Tone & Communication Guidelines
* **Personalization:** Always address the customer by their first name.
* **Professionalism:** Maintain an empathetic, professional, and non-dismissive tone at all times.
* **Clarity:** Use plain language; avoid internal jargon.
* **Declines:** If declining a request, clearly explain the policy reason and immediately offer the best available alternative.
* **Escalations:** Always keep the customer informed that their case is being reviewed by a specialist.
